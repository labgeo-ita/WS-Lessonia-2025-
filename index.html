About the Event

WS LESSONIA 2025 will bring together researchers from LabGeo (IEI-ITA) and LabGE (IEE-ITA) to discuss cutting-edge geospatial science using very-high spatial and temporal resolutions orbital Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) data from the ICEYE nano-satellite constellation.
